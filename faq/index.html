Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

You can find us by our link. It integrates smoothly with google maps and navigation to pin point our location with precision. We can't wait to see you.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

If your team seems a bit disjointed lately or needs to find the spirit and focus to have cohesive concepts, then Unplugged is for you.

What size teams can you accommodate?

Unplugged can receive teams up to 24 members. We ask that we have attendance forms filled out asap so team Unplugged can be ready to support you.

Is there really no wifi available?

At Unplugged we feel the focus should be on your team. However, we understand that there are situations that can't wait, so we have a fully function computer room at your disposal when business calls.
